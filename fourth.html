📜 The King's Reflection 📜

The fourth quarter was a battlefield, one filled with late nights, deadlines, and performances that pushed us beyond our limits. Like a hero lost at sea, I fought against the tides of assignments, only to ask myself, “Was it worth it?” The answer? Yes. Through struggles, I found camaraderie. Through challenges, I found growth. “I know that I’m meant for more” echoes in my mind as I navigate this journey, striving to prove that I am stronger than yesterday. The adventures of this quarter, though exhausting, taught me to endure, to learn, and to continue my odyssey, no matter how far from Ithaca I may be.
